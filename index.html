What is Adyakshar?

Adyakshar, a one if its kind innovation platform created by a group of accomplished leaders from technology, science and business, offers custom solutions for aspiring students, forward thinking high tech professionals and startup & midsize technology ventures. While entreprenenurial students and professionals can find unique career oriented training packages at Adyakshar, emerging ventures can find a suitable solution partner that can expedite their growth.
